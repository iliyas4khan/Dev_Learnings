Mahila Mitr

by iliyas khan

Swapn salona ati alaukik,
chandra sa badan tumhara priye,
mrignaini tum anant anupa,
chitt chahe aatm aalingan priye,
kesh jano sarso ki baali,
mohe bhramit kar jaati hai,
adhar tihare atyant nirale,
ye netr kuch kahna chahti hai,
duarpat kholo in aankho ka,
ye man chahe aarakshan priye,
swapn salona ati alaukik,
chandra sa badan tumahra priye!
